# TechCorp Company Policies

## Refund Policy

### Eligibility
Refunds are available under the following conditions:

**Monthly Subscriptions:**
- Full refund within 14 days of initial purchase
- Pro-rated refund within 14 days for plan upgrades
- No refund after 14 days

**Annual Subscriptions:**
- Full refund within 30 days of purchase
- Pro-rated refund between 30-90 days
- No refund after 90 days

**Enterprise Contracts:**
- As specified in individual contract terms
- Contact your account manager

### How to Request a Refund
1. Email billing@techcorp.com
2. Include:
   - Account email
   - Subscription details
   - Reason for refund request
3. Allow 3-5 business days for processing
4. Refunds issued to original payment method

### Non-Refundable Items
- Domain registrations
- SSL certificates
- Custom development work
- Overages and usage fees
- Add-on purchases after 7 days
