## Account Management

### How do I create an account?
To create a TechCorp account:
1. Visit www.techcorp.com/signup
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Verify your email through the confirmation link
4. Complete your profile information
5. Choose your subscription plan

### How do I reset my password?
To reset your password:
1. Go to www.techcorp.com/login
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for the reset link
5. Click the link and create a new password
6. Password must be at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols

### How do I update my billing information?
To update billing information:
1. Log into your account
2. Navigate to Settings > Billing
3. Click "Update Payment Method"
4. Enter your new card details
5. Click "Save Changes"

Note: Changes will apply to your next billing cycle.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your subscription:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Settings > Subscription
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Select a cancellation reason
5. Confirm cancellation

Note: You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation.

## Technical Support

### My application is running slowly. What should I do?
If you're experiencing slow performance:

1. **Check your resource usage**
   - Login to your dashboard
   - Navigate to Monitoring > Resources
   - Check CPU, memory, and network usage

2. **Clear cache and cookies**
   - Clear your browser cache
   - Clear application cache from Settings > Cache

3. **Check for updates**
   - Ensure you're using the latest version
   - Go to Settings > Updates

4. **Optimize your configuration**
   - Review your auto-scaling settings
   - Consider upgrading your plan if resources are maxed

5. **Contact support** if issues persist

### How do I integrate TechCorp API?
To integrate our API:

1. **Get your API key**
   - Navigate to Settings > API
   - Click "Generate New Key"
   - Copy and securely store your key

2. **Read the documentation**
   - Visit docs.techcorp.com/api
   - Review authentication methods
   - Explore available endpoints

3. **Test in sandbox**
   - Use our sandbox environment first
   - Base URL: sandbox-api.techcorp.com

4. **Example request:**
   ```
   curl -X GET https://api.techcorp.com/v1/data \
     -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"
   ```

### What should I do if I encounter an error?
When encountering an error:

1. **Note the error code and message**
   - Take a screenshot if possible
   - Note the time and what you were doing

2. **Check our status page**
   - Visit status.techcorp.com
   - Check for any ongoing incidents

3. **Search our knowledge base**
   - Visit help.techcorp.com
   - Search for the error code

4. **Contact support with:**
   - Error code and message
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Screenshot or screen recording
   - Your account email

## Security and Privacy

### How do you protect my data?
We protect your data through:

- **Encryption**: AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- **Access controls**: Role-based access with MFA
- **Regular audits**: SOC 2 Type II certified
- **Backups**: Daily automated backups with 30-day retention
- **Compliance**: GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA compliant

### How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?
To enable 2FA:
1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Choose your method:
   - Authenticator app (recommended)
   - SMS
4. Follow the setup instructions
5. Save your backup codes securely

### How do I report a security vulnerability?
To report security issues:
- Email: security@techcorp.com
- Use our responsible disclosure program
- Do not publicly disclose until resolved
- We offer a bug bounty program for valid reports

### How do I delete my account and data?
To delete your account:
1. Cancel your subscription first
2. Go to Settings > Account
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm by entering your password
5. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

You can request a data export before deletion.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### I can't log in to my account
If you can't log in:
1. Verify you're using the correct email
2. Check your Caps Lock key
3. Try resetting your password
4. Clear browser cookies
5. Try a different browser
6. Check if your account is locked (5 failed attempts)
7. Contact support if issues persist

### My emails aren't sending
If emails aren't sending:
1. Check your email quota (Settings > Email)
2. Verify recipient email addresses
3. Check spam/junk folders
4. Ensure your domain is properly verified
5. Check for bounce messages
6. Contact support with message IDs

### I'm receiving "Quota Exceeded" errors
If you've exceeded your quota:
1. Check current usage in Dashboard > Usage
2. Delete unnecessary files or data
3. Upgrade your plan for more storage
4. Enable auto-archiving for old data
5. Contact sales for custom solutions
